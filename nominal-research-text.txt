Executive Summary
What we did:
Our CRO research framework is a comprehensive research framework that provides a good balance between agility and
thoroughness. It includes a multitude of qualitative and quantitative analysis methods to extract the most value out of all
data sources. In this case the research methods used were: UX heuristic analysis, Google Analytics audit, heatmaps, exit
polls, user testing, and customer surveys.

What we found:
Our research has resulted in74 recommendations for improved user experience and conversion rate. These
opportunities centre around the following problem statements:
●
●
●
●

Category Navigation: Users find it difficult to navigation to a relevant product category
Information Gathering: Users struggled to determine which products is best for them
Delivery & Returns: Users have uncertainties around the delivery & returns process
Checkout: There is opportunity to streamline the checkout process.

Next Steps:
After presenting the key insights from each activity and the top priority recommendations we’ll transfer everything into a
prioritised database using our PX prioritisation model. This will inform development of a roadmap of ongoing
optimization activity to support you in achieving your goals.

Key Takeaways | Heatmap Analysis
1.

High interaction with the menu: As users move further down the funnel, menu and search
interactions increase, showing a strong intent to explore and compare options

2.

Interest in browsing options: Users engage more with collection cards than product cards, indicating
a preference to explore full collections before committing to a specific product. This behavior is also
reflected in navigation across multiple pages into collections.

3.

Strong engagement with the reviews section: Even located lower on the page section

4.

Interest in key benefits: Users frequently engage with the Palestine donation banner, material details,
and warranty information, showing these are important decision factors

5.

Potential in the "Perfectly Paired Pieces" section: Users show interest in product combinations and
spend time here, though they aren’t frequently clicking the bundle cart or SKU selectors to proceed.
There's opportunity to optimize this section to drive more action

goal?

We can improve how this content is displayed to better communicate our benefits, build trust, and engage the
community.
